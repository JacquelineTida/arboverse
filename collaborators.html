Our Team

William Marciel de Souza, PhD

Brazil

Universidadede São Paulo, Brazil

Dr. Souza is a FAPESP Postdoctoral researcher at the Virology Center Research, University of São Paulo, Brazil. He is a member of the International Committee of Taxonomy of Viruses (Peribunyaviridae and Hepeviridae Group). His work focuses on prospective, genomic and evolutionary studies of arboviruses and emerging zoonotic viruses.

Priscilla Paschoal Barbosa, MSc

Brazil

Univesidade Estadual de Campinas, Brazil

Priscilla is a PhD student at the University of Campinas (Brazil) in the Department of Genetics, Evolution, Microbiology and Immunology. For her dissertation, Priscilla will be standardizing a low-cost molecular diagnostic method for neglected arboviruses and conducting molecular epidemiology studies on arboviruses in the tri-border area (Brazil, Paraguay and Bolivia).
