Arboverse | The world of arbovirus

Our main role is to centralize the main arbovirus information around the world. And from this, we evaluate the impacts of this factor with other factors that are closely correlated with outbreaks of arbovirus and emergence of new arboviruses in all corners of the world. Through this way, it will be possible to view a complete catalog of all or arboviruses and their characteristics, to visualize on an interactive map the emergence and outbreak of arboviruses associated with several other factors and to view the history of arboviruses in an interactive timeline.
